Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer a free site survey?

Yes — for all security and POS projects we provide a free, no-obligation on-site survey to assess your requirements and provide an accurate quotation.

How quickly can you install?

Most standard installations are completed within 24–72 hours of the site survey and quote approval, subject to equipment availability.

Do you provide after-sales support?

Absolutely. All installations come with a warranty period and we offer Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) for ongoing 24/7 support and servicing.

What areas do you cover?

We currently serve clients across the city and surrounding regions. Contact us to confirm whether we cover your specific location — we're expanding rapidly.

Can you handle large enterprise projects?

Yes. We have successfully delivered multi-site enterprise projects including warehouse networks, hospital systems and hotel-wide fire safety installations.

What brands do you work with?

We partner with leading brands including Hikvision, Dahua, ZKTeco, Bosch, Epson, and more — ensuring premium quality at competitive prices.
